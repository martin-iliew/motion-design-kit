# Risk-Reversal CTA Band

**Framework:** HTML/CSS
**Category:** cta
**2026 Relevance:** The strongest landing pages still close with a CTA plus reassurance rather than a generic brand flourish.

## Description

Risk-Reversal CTA Band is a focused closing section that pairs the final action with reassurance like migration support, trial terms, or guided onboarding.

## Do's

- make the next step explicit
- attach one strong reassurance line
- keep the section visually simpler than the hero

## Don'ts

- do not end the page with vague slogan copy
- do not overload the band with secondary links
- do not repeat the hero without adding reassurance

## Best Practices

This section should convert hesitating buyers, not restate brand positioning.
