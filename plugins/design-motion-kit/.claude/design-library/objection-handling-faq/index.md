# Objection-Handling FAQ

**Framework:** HTML/CSS
**Category:** faq
**2026 Relevance:** FAQ sections remain valuable when they resolve sales friction instead of repeating help-center copy.

## Description

Objection-Handling FAQ answers rollout, pricing, migration, security, and support questions that typically block conversion.

## Do's

- write questions a buyer would actually ask
- keep answers concise
- use the section to remove friction late in the page

## Don'ts

- do not dump support trivia
- do not hide important pricing rules elsewhere
- do not let answers grow into documentation articles

## Best Practices

Every answer should reduce hesitation or explain the next step.
