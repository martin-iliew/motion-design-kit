# Grid-Led Hero

**Framework:** HTML/CSS
**Category:** hero
**2026 Relevance:** Still the safest high-performing default because major product-company landing pages keep pairing a disciplined grid with immediate proof and product context.

## Description

Grid-Led Hero uses a clear two-column desktop layout with left-aligned messaging, an explicit CTA cluster, and a right-side product frame or visual anchor.

## Do's

- lead with a concrete headline and specific CTA
- show real product context, not abstract filler
- place proof directly under the CTA group

## Don'ts

- do not center everything unless the product genuinely benefits from a billboard hero
- do not let decorative art outrank product understanding
- do not bury trust below the fold

## Best Practices

Treat this as the default homepage hero for serious product companies. The power comes from disciplined information order, not visual stunts.
