# Customer Story Metrics

**Framework:** HTML/CSS
**Category:** case-study
**2026 Relevance:** Current serious landing pages increasingly bridge customer proof and metrics in the same block instead of separating them into isolated sections.

## Description

Customer Story Metrics pairs a short customer narrative with one or more quantified outcomes and brief implementation context.

## Do's

- name the customer or customer type
- make the result measurable
- keep the story short and specific

## Don'ts

- do not use vanity metrics with no context
- do not turn the section into a full case-study article
- do not duplicate the same metrics elsewhere without a reason

## Best Practices

This is one of the strongest mid-page trust sections because it ties promise to proof without killing momentum.
